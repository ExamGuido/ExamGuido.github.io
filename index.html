JEE Admit Cards are out now!!! Check it Out! NEW

JEE Main 2022 - NTA has issued the session 1 JEE Main admit card 2022 on June 21.

Appearing candidates can download JEE Mains 2022 admit card in online mode. The authorities will conduct the JEE Main 2022 session 1 from June 23 to 29. The JEE Main admit card download link for session 1 is activated at jeemain.nta.nic.in. Students have to use their JEE Main 2022 login credentials to download IIT JEE Mains admit card 2022. The authorities have started the JEE Mains session 2 registration at jeemain.nta.nic.in 2022. The JEE Main 2022 application form session 2 is available online from June 1 to 30. The JEE Main 2022 session 2 will be held from July 21, 22, 23, 24, 25, 26, 27, 28, 29 and 30.
